## 2026-01-14 - Expanding Touch Targets with Labels
**Learning:** Wrapping toggle switches and their description text in a `<label>` element significantly improves usability by creating a larger hit area, especially for touch users, while automatically associating the text with the control for accessibility.
**Action:** When implementing settings or list items with a single interactive control, wrap the entire container in a `<label>` and add `cursor-pointer`.
